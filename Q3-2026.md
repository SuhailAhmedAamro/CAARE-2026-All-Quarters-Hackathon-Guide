- **Spatial AI**: Robot ko estimate karna ke koi object kitni door hai.

### 5. Simulation with NVIDIA Isaac Sim
- **Photorealistic 3D environment** mein robot ko train karna before real hardware purchase.

### 6. Edge AI Deployment
- AI model ko direct robot ke processor par run karna (Latency kam karne ke liye).

## üèÜ Q3 Hackathon: "The Physical AI Challenge"

### Goal
- Autonomous digital agents ko **physical world tasks** solve karne ke liye design karna.

### Example Tasks
- **Autonomous Delivery Bot**: Isaac Sim mein rasta dhund kar parcel A se B tak deliver kare (Obstacle Avoidance).  
- **Smart Sorting Arm**: Robotic arm camera use karke trash aur recyclables alag kare.  
- **Humanoid Voice Commander**: Unitree Go2 robot ko Urdu commands dena aur execute karna ("Agay chalo", "Baith jao").

### Grading Criteria
- **Control Accuracy**: Robot ne task kitni safai se complete kiya? (40%)  
- **Vision Integration**: AI ne cheezon ko sahi pehchana? (30%)  
- **Real-time Response**: Command aur robot movement ke beech latency kitni thi? (20%)  
- **Spec-Compliance**: Governess policies aur hardware safety (10%)
